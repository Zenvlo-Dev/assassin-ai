Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 26, 2026 | Effective Date: August 1, 2026

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Assassin AI ("the Bot," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the personal data you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and safeguard it, and what rights you have concerning your information.

Assassin AI follows a privacy-by-design philosophy: we only store the minimum data required to deliver our services. We do not sell your data, and we never use it for advertising or profiling purposes.

By using Assassin AI, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please remove the Bot from your server immediately.

Data We Collect

To provide our features, Assassin AI collects and stores the following categories of data:

2.1 Discord Identifiers

  • User IDs — Unique Discord snowflake IDs for users who interact with the Bot's features (warnings, tickets, giveaways, reputation, etc.)
  • Server (Guild) IDs — Unique identifiers for servers where the Bot is present, used to store per-server configuration
  • Channel IDs — Identifiers for configured log channels, ticket categories, and announcement channels
  • Role IDs — Identifiers for auto-roles, temporary roles, restricted roles, and moderator roles

2.2 Configuration Data

  • Moderation settings (automod thresholds, word filters, punishment levels)
  • Ticket system configurations (categories, support roles, panels)
  • Giveaway settings (requirements, cooldowns, prize formats)
  • Logging preferences (what events are tracked and where)
  • Custom command definitions and auto-response triggers
  • Welcome and goodbye message templates

2.3 Moderation History

  • Warnings — Records of warnings issued to users, including reason, issuing moderator, timestamp, and appeal status
  • Mutes, Kicks, and Bans — Logs of enforcement actions taken by the Bot
  • Reputation Scores — Escalation counts tied to automated action thresholds

2.4 Ticket Content

  • Ticket transcripts containing user messages exchanged within ticket channels
  • File attachments submitted through the ticket system (transcripts, evidence)
  • Ticket metadata (creation time, closing time, assigned staff, status)

2.5 Giveaway Entries

  • User IDs of participants entering giveaways
  • Winner selection records for audit and dispute resolution

2.6 Temporary Role Assignments

  • User IDs, role IDs, assignment timestamps, and calculated expiration timestamps

2.7 Audit Log Events

  • Server events the Bot is configured to track (member joins, deletions, moderation actions)

2.8 Interaction Statistics

  • Aggregated anonymous usage statistics (command execution counts, feature adoption rates, error frequencies) used exclusively for improving the Bot. These statistics cannot identify individual users or servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Transparency is key. Assassin AI expressly does not collect:

  • Message content — Messages are only processed ephemerally for moderation and are not permanently stored
  • Personal contact information — Email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses (unless voluntarily submitted via email for support)
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • IP addresses — Beyond what is inherently logged by our cloud infrastructure provider
  • Biometric data
  • Payment card details — All payments are processed through third-party payment processors; we never see or store your full financial details
  • Private Discord messages — Unless forwarded through the DM Relay feature with explicit user consent

Why We Collect This Data

Each piece of data serves a clear functional purpose:

  • Discord IDs — Essential for associating actions with users and servers, enforcing permissions, and maintaining configuration
  • Configuration data — Allows server administrators to customize the Bot's behavior to fit their community's needs
  • Moderation history — Enables progressive discipline, automated escalation, and appeal processes
  • Ticket transcripts — Provides accountability, record-keeping, and dispute resolution
  • Giveaway records — Ensures fair winner selection and prevents abuse
  • Temporary role data — Automates role management for time-limited scenarios
  • Analytics — Helps us understand usage patterns, prioritize features, and diagnose issues

How We Store and Secure Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest — All stored data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Encryption in transit — All network communication uses TLS 1.3
  • Cloudflare infrastructure — Data is hosted on Cloudflare's globally distributed network, protected by DDoS mitigation, Web Application Firewall (WAF), and bot management
  • Access controls — Only designated development team members have access to production databases, and access is logged and audited
  • Regular backups — Encrypted backups are performed periodically for disaster recovery

Despite these measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to improve our protections.

Data Retention Periods

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose:

  • Server configuration — Retained until the Bot is removed from the server, plus 30 days
  • Warning and moderation records — Retained until manually purged by server admins or 12 months after removal of the Bot
  • Ticket transcripts — Retained indefinitely for accountability, or until deletion is specifically requested
  • Giveaway records — Retained for 90 days after giveaway completion
  • Temporary role assignments — Deleted automatically upon role expiration or Bot removal
  • Aggregated analytics — Retained indefinitely in anonymized form

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

6.1 Right to Access

You may request a copy of the data we hold about you or your server. Contact us through our support server, and we will respond within 30 days.

6.2 Right to Rectification

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

6.3 Right to Deletion ("Right to Be Forgotten")

You may request deletion of your data. Note that deleting certain data (such as warning records) may affect moderation functionality. We will honor deletion requests within 30 days unless we are legally obligated to retain the data.

6.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You may request that we limit how your data is processed while a complaint is investigated.

6.5 Right to Data Portability

You may request a structured, machine-readable copy of your data.

6.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw consent for data processing at any time by removing the Bot from your server. Some data (such as aggregated statistics) that has already been processed may remain.

6.7 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through:

  • Discord: Join our official support server and open a privacy-related ticket
  • Email: privacy@assassin-ai.com 📋

We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Third-Party Services

Assassin AI relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each service has its own privacy practices:

  • Discord Inc. — The Bot operates through Discord's API. Your use of Discord is governed by Discord's Privacy Policy. Discord receives data whenever you interact with the Bot.
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — We use Cloudflare Workers for serverless computation and Cloudflare's global network for content delivery and security. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.
  • Stripe (if applicable) — Payment processing for Premium subscriptions is handled by Stripe. We do not store full payment card information. View Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

The Assassin AI website (assassin-ai.github.io) uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for website functionality (e.g., session management, CSRF tokens). No user tracking.
  • Analytics cookies — We may use aggregated analytics (e.g., Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand page traffic. These are privacy-preserving and do not identify individual visitors.

The Bot itself does not use cookies. You can configure your browser to reject cookies, though some website features may not function properly.

Children's Privacy

Assassin AI is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete that information. Where required, we will obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting data from minors.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide information about the nature and scope of the breach
  • Outline steps taken to mitigate the breach and prevent recurrence
  • Notify relevant supervisory authorities as required by applicable law

Notifications will be sent via announcements in our Discord support server and, where feasible, direct messaging.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced through:

  • A post in our Discord support server
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

We encourage you to review this Policy periodically. Your continued use of Assassin AI after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
