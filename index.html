FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Assassin AI is a premium all-in-one Discord bot that provides AI-powered moderation, a professional ticket system, giveaways, reputation tracking, logging, temporary roles, and dozens of other powerful management tools — all in one seamless package.
Assassin AI offers a generous free tier with core features including basic moderation, limited tickets, and standard utilities. Premium plans unlock AI AutoMod, unlimited tickets, priority support, advanced giveaways, and full dashboard access.
Our AI moderation uses a neural network trained on millions of Discord messages to accurately detect toxic content, spam, phishing links, and harassment. Unlike keyword filters, it understands context and can detect evasive language with over 97% accuracy.
Assassin AI only temporarily processes messages for moderation purposes. Message content is not permanently stored. We store only the minimum information required — server IDs, user IDs, configuration settings, ticket transcripts, and moderation history.
Setup is simple! Use the /panel command to create a ticket panel, customize the embed with /panel edit, and configure categories. Users can then open tickets by clicking buttons or selecting from dropdown menus. Full documentation is available in our support server.
Yes! Our support team can help you migrate your existing configuration, warnings, and ticket history from other popular bots. Join our support server and open a ticket to get started with migration assistance.
Assassin AI requires standard moderation permissions (Manage Messages, Kick, Ban, Moderate Members) along with Manage Channels for ticket creation and Manage Roles for automation. We recommend the Administrator permission for full functionality, but you can restrict to specific permissions as needed.
Join our official support server at discord.gg/2dgn5wCK8z and open a ticket. Our team typically responds within hours. You can also check our documentation for guides and troubleshooting tips.
